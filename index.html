Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the loyalty program

How do I earn loyalty points?

+

You can earn loyalty points through various activities:

  • Making purchases online or in-store at partner retailers
  • Referring friends to join the program
  • Participating in special promotions and campaigns
  • Writing product reviews and sharing on social media

Points are typically awarded within 24-48 hours of a qualifying transaction.

Do my points expire?

+

Points have a 2-year expiry period from the date they are earned. You will receive email notifications 30 days before any points are set to expire, giving you time to redeem them.

How can I redeem my points?

+

You can redeem points in several ways:

  • Online through our website or mobile app
  • At physical store locations using your membership card
  • Convert to gift cards for popular retailers
  • Apply as discounts during checkout
  • Exchange for exclusive products and experiences

What's the minimum redemption amount?

+

The minimum redemption amount is 100 points, which equals ‚Çπ10 in value. This ensures that all members can enjoy the benefits regardless of their point balance.

How do I link my online and offline purchases?

+

Your account automatically links purchases through:

  • Your registered email address or phone number
  • QR code scanning at participating stores
  • NFC-enabled loyalty card
  • Mobile app check-ins at partner locations

All transactions appear in your unified dashboard within minutes.

Can I transfer points to another account?

+

Points are non-transferable between accounts for security reasons. However, you can gift rewards redeemed with your points to friends and family.

Why aren't my points showing up?

+

If your points aren't appearing:

  • Wait 24-48 hours as processing can take time
  • Ensure you're logged into the correct account
  • Check if the merchant is a participating partner
  • Verify your purchase meets minimum requirements
  • Contact support if points still don't appear after 48 hours

Is my personal information secure?

+

Yes, we take security seriously. We use enterprise-grade encryption, comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations, and never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. All transactions are secured with industry-standard protocols.
